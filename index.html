About Us

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

A Filipino nation, unified by a deep sense of pride in their common identity, cultural heritage and natural patrimony, and imbibed with the spirit of nationalism and strong commitment to the protection and dissemination of legacy.

Mission

The primary mission of the National Museum of the Philippines is to acquire, document, preserve, exhibit, and foster scholarly study and appreciation of works of art, specimens, and cultural and historical artifacts.

Mandate & Objectives

Appreciation of the collections and research findings of See-ning, as well as technical and museological skills and knowledge, are disseminated through exhibitions, publications, and educational, training, outreach, technical assistance and other public programs.
